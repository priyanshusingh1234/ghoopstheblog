## 3. Classic American Cheeseburger üçî

**Origin:** USA  
**Prep Time:** 10 mins  
**Cook Time:** 10 mins  
**Serves:** 4

### Ingredients

- 500g ground beef (80/20 lean)
- Salt and pepper
- 4 burger buns
- 4 slices of cheddar cheese
- Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles
- Ketchup, mustard, mayo
- Butter for toasting buns

### Instructions

1. **Shape ground beef** into 4 patties. Season with salt and pepper.
2. **Grill or pan-fry** the patties over medium-high heat for about 4 minutes per side.
3. **Add cheese** to the patties in the last minute of cooking.
4. **Toast buns** lightly with butter in a pan.
5. **Assemble burgers** with lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments of your choice.

### Why It‚Äôs Great

Few foods are as universally loved as the cheeseburger. This classic is juicy, flavorful, and customizable to your liking ‚Äî a true American staple.

## 4. Thai Green Curry üç≤

**Origin:** Thailand  
**Prep Time:** 15 mins  
**Cook Time:** 20 mins  
**Serves:** 4

### Ingredients

- 400g chicken breast, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp green curry paste
- 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 cup bamboo shoots or bell peppers
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- Thai basil leaves
- Lime wedges
- Cooked jasmine rice

### Instructions

1. **Heat curry paste** in a pot with a little oil for 1 minute.
2. Add **coconut milk and chicken**, cook until chicken is almost done.
3. Pour in **chicken broth**, add vegetables, sugar, and fish sauce.
4. Simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce is slightly thickened.
5. **Serve hot** with jasmine rice and a sprinkle of Thai basil.

### Why It‚Äôs Great

Thai Green Curry is a perfect balance of spicy, sweet, and savory. It‚Äôs aromatic, exotic, and easy to prepare at home with the right ingredients.
